My vibrant soul was born on June 4, 2001, in the enchanting land of Georgetown, Guyana. At the tender age of three, I embarked on a life-altering journey, immigrating to a new world when I was just three, leaving behind the familiar rhythms of home. I was sandwiched between a little brother, who's five years my junior, and an older sister, seven years my senior—our house was always buzzing with dreams and laughter. An artistic spirit bloomed early in me, finding solace and joy in the strokes of my paintbrush, particularly when crafting captivating portraits. Fueling my insatiable curiosity, I embraced the thrill of experimentation, relishing the challenge of trying new things. Orange hues swirled into the canvas of my life as her favorite color, and I'm currently reveling in her "My Pink Girl era," a phase marked by self-discovery and self-development.

High school days witnessed Tashea's dynamic presence on The Step & Dance team, where her rhythmic prowess and infectious energy left an indelible mark. Her professional journey commenced in the construction realm, initially aspiring to be a civil engineer before pivoting towards project management. A detour caused by a car accident led her to a stint in security, but the longing for fulfillment tugged at her spirit. Tashea boldly switched lanes, setting her sights on one of her lifelong aspirations—computer programming. Enrolling in a software development BootCamp, she now dreams of breaking into the tech world, aspiring to achieve financial freedom and create a legacy of entrepreneurship that includes businesses, properties, and all things dear to her eclectic heart.

Beyond her tech pursuits, Tashea's multifaceted identity shines through in her love for hairstyling, makeup artistry, and a kaleidoscope of musical genres—rock, punk, rap, jazz, classical, and the sweet harmonies of R&B. Amidst this diverse soundscape, her heart remains entwined with Harry Styles and the evergreen allure of One Direction, sparking spontaneous car jams and dance parties that echo the unbridled joy of her youth. Tashea Lynnelle, a woman on a quest for freedom, fulfillment, and the limitless hues of her dreams.
